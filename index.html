Girls Who Code is a social and professional club that strives to connect girls and help them further their computer science skills. Our goals include creating a safe space to teach girls about computer science along with working towards closing the gender gap in technology by inspiring others to learn computer science.

We envision teaching girls from all majors and backgrounds. We want every girl to have an opportunity to learn computer science and utilize this knowledge to make an impact on the world in their own way.
